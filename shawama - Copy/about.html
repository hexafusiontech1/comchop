Our Story

At Shawama Nigeria, we’re more than just a food brand—we’re a celebration of bold flavors, vibrant culture, and the joy of sharing meals. Whether it’s a sizzling shawarma, a premium bottle of wine, or a luxurious cake, we’re here to delight your senses.

Founded with the mission to bring restaurant-quality food to your doorstep, we pride ourselves on using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and maintaining world-class hygiene standards. Each product reflects our commitment to excellence and love for culinary artistry.

Our goal is to inspire connection through food—whether you’re celebrating, relaxing, or simply craving comfort. Let’s make every meal a memory.
